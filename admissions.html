Admissions Process

Begin your journey with Sanidhya Education

Welcome Future Students

At Sanidhya Education, we seek curious, motivated students who will thrive in our challenging academic environment and contribute to our vibrant community. Our admissions process is designed to help us get to know each applicant while providing families with all the information they need.

We accept applications year-round, but priority consideration is given to those who apply by our January 15 deadline for the following academic year.

Our Admissions Process

1

Inquiry

Submit an inquiry form or attend an open house to learn more about our programs.

2

Application

Complete our online application and submit required documents.

3

Assessment

Students participate in age-appropriate assessments or interviews.

4

Decision

Admissions decisions are communicated within 2 weeks of assessment.

Admissions by Age Group

Pre-Primary (Ages 3-5)

  • Play-based assessment
  • Parent interview
  • Birth certificate required
  • Priority given to siblings

Elementary (Grades 1-5)

  • Academic assessment
  • Previous school records
  • Teacher recommendation
  • Classroom visit

Middle & High School (Grades 6-12)

  • Entrance exams in core subjects
  • Transcripts from current school
  • Student essay
  • Family interview

Frequently Asked Questions

When should we begin the application process?

+

We recommend beginning the application process at least 3 months before your desired start date. For the August intake, applications open the previous September with a priority deadline of January 15.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

+

Our average student-teacher ratio is 15:1 across all grade levels. In Pre-Primary, the ratio is 10:1 with a teacher and assistant in each classroom.

Do you offer transportation services?

+

Yes, we have a network of school buses serving major areas of Kathmandu Valley. Transportation fees vary based on distance and are billed separately from tuition.

Are uniforms required?

+

Yes, all students are required to wear the Sanidhya Education uniform which can be purchased through our school office. We have separate uniforms for summer and winter seasons.
