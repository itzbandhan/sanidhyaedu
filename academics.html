Curriculum Highlights

STEM Education

Our STEM program integrates Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics through hands-on learning and real-world applications.

  • State-of-the-art science labs
  • Robotics and coding classes
  • Annual science fair
  • Partnerships with tech companies

Humanities

Our humanities program develops critical thinking, cultural awareness, and effective communication skills.

  • World literature studies
  • Model United Nations
  • Debate and public speaking
  • Cultural exchange programs

Visual & Performing Arts

We nurture creativity and self-expression through comprehensive arts education.

  • Music theory and performance
  • Studio art and digital design
  • Theater productions
  • Annual art exhibition

Athletics & Wellness

Our sports program promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and healthy competition.

  • Olympic-size swimming pool
